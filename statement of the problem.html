STATEMENT OF THE PROBLEM



This study aimed to determine the impact of an eco-friendly and sustainable backpack in the environment and to the drafting students of Marikina Polytechnic College. Specifically, it sought to answer the following questions:


1. How may an eco-friendly and sustainable backpack be described as the best bag for drafting students?


2. What are the common problems the drafting students of Marikina Polytechnic College are confronting in terms of


1.1 Equipment


1.2 Materials


3. Does an eco-friendly and sustainable backpack have significant impact on drafting students?
