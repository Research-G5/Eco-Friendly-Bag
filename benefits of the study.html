BENEFITS OF THE STUDY



The researchers believe that this study will not only yield data that will be helpful to drafting students, but more so to the following groups of people:


The Researchers. The main researchers will definitely benefit from this study, for the reason that they are continuously learning things as they go along with it.


The Drafting Students. They are the main beneficiaries of the study. The eco-friendly backpack will help them organize and get all their stuff easily with its compartments. In addition, academic supplies and drafting equipment and materials can be conveniently stored in the same bag.


The Drafting Professors. The result of this study will serve as information for them. They may also use it as a basis for their studies. Thus, the said professors also play a vital role in this study.


Future Researchers. The result of this study could also be beneficial to them due to the fact that they may use the information and ideas from this study to improve their own.
