THEORETICAL FRAMEWORK
OF THE STUDY


This study is anchored on the theoretical support of the model of the components of the product. The term product before is defined as something produced by businesses. The theory defines it as something convenient to the consumers, the way they are satisfied and that satisfaction is not only physical but also psychological. Thus, a product should include more elements, a theory from (Chunawalla, 2017).

The eco-friendly and sustainable backpack meets the impacts of the model. It has physical attributes that might boost the confidence of the wearer or drafting student. It can also benefit the students psychologically. The product contains special features, which are its compartments and guarantee.






The hierarchy table shows the impact of an eco-friendly backpack on three (3) categories.. The first category is the advantages of the product. Versatility refers to the compartments of the backpack that arrange academic supplies and drafting tools in an organized way. Sustainability is the ability of the product to carry stuff for a long period of time, and hassle-free refers to drafting students who will no longer need other carrying tools for drafting since the equipment and materials will be stored in the same bag.

Another category is about the benefits of the backpack. drafting students as the main beneficiaries of the study. The eco-friendly backpack will help them organize and get all their stuff easily with its compartments. In addition, academic supplies and drafting equipment and materials can be conveniently stored in the same bag. Drafting professors can also benefit from it because the result of this study will serve as information for them. They may also use it as a basis for their studies. It also benefits the environment since the product causes less harm as compared to a typical backpack.
The last category refers to the drawbacks of the product. Its heaviness is the main issue with the backpack, followed by some illnesses a student may get from carrying the product. It will also not be completely eco-friendly.
